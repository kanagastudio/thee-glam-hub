📌 beginner steps

  • start with clean skin before applying makeup
  • ALWAYSSSS moisturize your face
  • skin prep well & use a damp beauty sponge/fingers to blend your base for a flawless finish
more beginner tips
  • moisturize your face lightly before applying anything on your face
  • use a lipliner if your lip shade doesn't match the lip gloss/stick/etc.
  • use natural light when doing makeup for best color match
  • use a lip balm to moisturize your lips before applying a matte lipstick/lip

🌞 skincare prep

  • cleanse🧼 — pick a gentle cleanser for your skin type
  • tone🍃 — helps balance and prep your skin
  • hydrate💦 — lightweight moisturizer works best under makeup
why skincare prep matters

proper prep creates a smooth canvas, helping your makeup last longer and look fresh all day. It also avoids skin from breaking out, and maintains the skin health!

🧡 brown-skin tips

  • Choose warm undertones but avoid anything that looks too orange
  • Use highlighter shades that add warmth, avoid greyish contour tones
  • Be mindful of SPF flashback in photos — test before buying!
pro tip

warm golds and bronzes can beautifully enhance brown skin tones. Try layering products for a natural glow.
